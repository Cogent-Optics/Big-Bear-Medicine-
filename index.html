💡 How Sacred Gathering Scheduling Works:

Each gathering shows 5 lunar-aligned dates. Circle members select Yes/Maybe/No for each day. Colors indicate: Green (4+ Yes), Purple (4+ Yes/Maybe), Orange (Less than 4). Click a date to select it for your gathering. The randomizer ensures fair rotation of sacred roles among available members.
